## Carrier Standard Provisions Quick Reference

### Citizens Property Insurance Corporation
- **Form:** HO-3 (standard homeowners)
- **Hurricane Deductible:** 2% of Coverage A (standard)
- **Water Damage:** $10,000 sublimit if NOT using Managed Repair Contractor Network; $3,000 emergency removal included
- **Managed Repair:** Free emergency water removal program; choosing managed repair = no sublimit on water
- **Assessments:** Policyholders may owe additional assessments after catastrophic events
- **RSPS:** Yes, via Managed Repair provisions
- **Key Risk:** Water damage cap if insured declined managed repair

### Peoples Trust Insurance
- **Form:** HO-3
- **Hurricane Deductible:** 2% of Coverage A
- **Roof Deductible:** Separate roof deductible (equal to hurricane deductible)
- **Preferred Contractor:** Endorsement reduces premium; may affect claim handling
- **Ordinance/Law:** 25% of Coverage A
- **Fungi/Mold:** $10,000 sublimit
- **Water Backup:** $5,000 endorsement
- **Wind Mitigation:** Major credits available (up to $5,138 in Williams case)
- **Key Risk:** Dual deductible (hurricane + roof); preferred contractor requirements

### Slide Insurance Company
- **Form:** HO-3
- **Hurricane Deductible:** 2% of Coverage A
- **AOP Deductible:** $500 (lowest in portfolio)
- **Water Damage:** $10,000 Limited Water Damage Coverage
- **Personal Property:** RCV endorsement available (+$389/yr)
- **Ordinance/Law:** 25% of Coverage A
- **Key Risk:** Low AOP deductible is favorable; water sublimit same as Citizens

### American Traditions Insurance Company
